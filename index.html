The Magic Ball

There are 6 different types of snow balls. They are in total 100 balls. The Twilight pony is trying to avoid a ball hit. If pony succeeds to avoid 70 hits, the game is won and the town will be saved from the hard winter.

How to play:

There are five balls with different colors: The blue one is increasing the speed of the ball by 2 points The green one is slowing down the speed of the ball by 2 pointss The purple one is decreasing the points by 10 The yellow one in increasing the points by 10

The control keys are:

Left arrow – move on left Right arrow – move on right Up arrow – move on up Down arrow – move on down
